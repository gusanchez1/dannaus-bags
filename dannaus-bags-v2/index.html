Our Story

Born from

Dannaus Bags was born from a friendship and a shared obsession with beautiful leather goods. Named after the elegant Danaus butterfly, we believe a great bag — like a butterfly — transforms the ordinary into something extraordinary.

Every piece in our collection is handpicked. We work closely with trusted suppliers to bring you 100% genuine leather bags that are one-of-a-kind — because you deserve something as unique as you are.
